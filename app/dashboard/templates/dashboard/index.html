Air Quality Monitors

Thanks to funding from Patagonia, the Environmental Law & Policy Center (ELPC) and community partners are collecting data with AirBeam monitors, created by the Brooklyn-based nonprofit HabitatMap.

Sampling Chicago's Air Quality

This video shows the percent of unhealthy monitor readings. The more red the color, the higher the percent of unhealthy measurements in that area. The height of the data illustrates the number of measurements collected in that area. So, the taller, redder columns are where we can say with some certainty that air quality is poor.
